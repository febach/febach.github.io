About Me

I am Dr. Felix Bach, a scientist with a research focus on the digitalisation of science. Currently, I serve as the Head of the Department "Research Data" at FIZ Karlsruhe and am the Co-Spokesperson of NFDI4Chem. Additionally, I am the scientific coordinator of the Leibniz WissenschaftsCampus DiTraRe (Digital Transformation of Research).

Professional Roles

  • Head of the Department "Research Data" at FIZ Karlsruhe
  • Co-Spokesperson of NFDI4Chem
  • Scientific Coordinator of Leibniz WissenschaftsCampus DiTraRe

Research Focus

My research is centred on the digitalisation of scientific processes and data management, aiming to enhance the accessibility and usability of research data in the scientific community.
