About Me

I am a scientist with a research focus on the digitalisation of science. Currently, I serve as the Head of the Department "Research Data" at FIZ Karlsruhe, where we do research and development in the field of research data infrastructure, involving a lot of software development and also service operation (e.g. for our research data repository RADAR). I am one of the Co-Spokespersons of NFDI4Chem - the chemistry consortium in the NFDI, the German research data infrastructure, in which I am also active in many other consortia, e.g. NFDI4Culture, NFDI4Memory and NFDI4Objects. Additionally, I am the scientific coordinator of the Leibniz WissenschaftsCampus DiTraRe (Digital Transformation of Research). I'm also an artist and musician, but that's another story and I will focus on the research part here.

Professional Roles

  • Head of the Department "Research Data" at FIZ Karlsruhe
  • Co-Spokesperson of NFDI4Chem (responsible for the task area repositories)
  • Scientific Coordinator of Leibniz WissenschaftsCampus DiTraRe

Research Focus

My research is centred on the digitalisation of scientific processes and data management, aiming to enhance the accessibility and usability of research data in the scientific community.
